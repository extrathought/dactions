Challenges

1

Rethink — Design a safe and flexible way for pedestrians to cross a street at any location. Find a balance to give pedestrians higher priority, discourage jaywalking but maintain traffic flow. Bonus points: Feasible solutions.

2

Redesign — Design a unique symbol representing user handles. Twitter and Facebook use @, Google uses +. The & represents "and", the @ is for emails, the © for "copyright" and $, £, € for currencies. Create a symbol that represents a person, a user, a human. Bonus points: Extensible symbol set for networks.

3

Refine — Design your perfect public mall toilet. Improve hygiene, cleanliness and privacy. Bonus points: Your perfect bathroom vanity spot.

Tuesday, July 19, 2011 at 19:00 at Artery, Red Dot Traffic Building, 28 Maxwell Road, Singapore. Pictures are here.
