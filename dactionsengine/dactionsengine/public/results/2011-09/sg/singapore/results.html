Challenges

1

Rethink — Radically redesign ONE of your favourite household items to work better, be more resourceful, elegant, to create a certain experience, be used in a different context... Kudos if you can change our most basic item.

2

Redesign — Design a new brand image and marketing strategy for North Korean tourism. Kudos if you can come up with an idea that improves the life of the common people.

3

Refine — Redesign the public Singapore MRT or bus route signage to be more useful for locals and visitors. Facilitate finding destinations off the routes and ease the dilemma of figuring out multi- connection trips. Kudos if your solution scales to the whole city and can be printed on sign-boards.

Tuesday, September 20, 2011 at 19:00 at Artery, Red Dot Traffic Building, 28 Maxwell Road, Singapore..
