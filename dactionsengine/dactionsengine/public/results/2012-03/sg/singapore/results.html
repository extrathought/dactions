Challenges

1

Phantasise — Design changes to our public spaces that allow animals from the zoo to roam our urban habitat freely. Find a way that makes it possible to let wild animals roam our city safely next to humans (e.g. Orchard Road area). Bonus: Any solution that allows us to get into arm's reach of a real lion.

2

Conceptualise — Design an elegant piece of men's clothing that allows provides warmth in freezing (AC) temperatures and that does not get in the way with hot and humid weather (i.e. does not have to be carried in one's hand or uses lots of space in a bag). Bonus: Style and functionality.

3

Visualise — Design icons that clearly visually represent the concepts of today, yesterday and tomorrow without using any text or letters. Bonus: Icons that work independent of culture.

Tuesday, March 20, 2012 at 19:00 at The Pigeonhole, 52/53 Duxton Road, Singapore.
