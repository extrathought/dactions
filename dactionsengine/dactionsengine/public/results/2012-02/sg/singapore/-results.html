Challenges

1

Rethink — Design a passport for a globalised world. The passports we use are from the last century and a physical world connected by land and sea. Create an object of identification that avoids the drawbacks of the well-know booklet. Bonus: Allow the owners the joy of collecting immigration stamps.

2

Redesign — Design your ideal café interior. Cafes serve different purposes such as relaxing, reading, socialising, working or thinking. What layout, furniture, services, etc. makes your ideal café? Bonus: While versatility is key, define one clear purpose for your café and design for that.

3

Refine — Design the ideal online service sign-up process. We sign up to new services almost every day from browsers and apps in computers, phones, tablets and more... What's easiest process for users to sign up while avoiding problems such as spam, identity theft or lost passwords? Bonus: Allow finding friends and connecting to them easy.

Tuesday, February 21, 2012 at 19:00 at The Pigeonhole, 52/53 Duxton Road, Singapore.
