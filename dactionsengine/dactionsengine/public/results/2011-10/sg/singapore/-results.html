Challenges

Rethink — Design a value-added service for public libraries outside of their core competence. Kudos for a generic and replicable service that cannot be done better online.

2

Redesign — Design a solution that cleans one's mailbox by making online notifications (Facebook, Google+, Twitter, passwords, etc.) easier to manage. Kudos for a solution that is implementable in parallel to one's current e-mail setup.

3

Refine — Design your ideal bag: Hand bag, laptop bag, shopping bag, travel bag, air bag... Kudos for style and feasibility.
